Uhlobo Lokuthatha Isampula yilo olusho ukuthi abanelayisensi banenkululeko engakanani. Eyokuthatha Isampula ivumela kuphela ukuba kusetshenziswe izingxenye ezithile futhi iyakwenqabela ukuba usetshenziswe wonke umsebenzi. Eyokuthatha Isampula Nokunye, ivumela abanamalayisensi ukuba bathathe futhi bashintshe izingxenye ezithile zomsebenzi, futhi bahlanganyela wonke umsebenzi ngenjongo engeyona eyezentengiselwano. Eyokuthatha Isampula Nokunye Okungekona Okwezentengiselwano iphinde ivimbele ilayisensi Yokuthatha iSampula Nokunye ngokunikeza imvume kuphela ukuba kwakhiwe okungekona okwezentengiselwano ezingxenyeni zomsebenzi wakho.
