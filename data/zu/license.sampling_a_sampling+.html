Ukuthatha Isampula Nokunye: Abantu bangase bathathe futhi bashintshe izingxenye ezithile zomsebenzi wakho ngenjongo ethile ngaphandle kokukhangisa, okuyinto eyenqatshelwe. Ukukopisha nokunikeza abanye (njengokuhlanganyela amafayela) umsebenzi wonke kodwa ungenzeli ukuthola inzalo nakho kuvunyelwe. Yingakho kuthiwa, "nokunye".
