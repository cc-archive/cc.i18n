Nansi indlela okusebenza ngayo: Umuntu uchofoza isithonjana se-Creative Commons athole "iMvume ye-Commons yakho" — uhlelo olukwazi ukufundwa umuntu lwelayisensi. Kusukela lapho usengaya "kwiKhodi Yomthetho" — ilayisensi yangempela ofake kulo okuqukethwe. Okufihlwe kule thegi "iKhodi Engafundwa Umshini" — i-metadata — ezokwenza abanye bakwazi ukuthola okuqukethwe kwakho. Umfanekiso wale nqubo ungatholakala kumakhomikhi ethu.
