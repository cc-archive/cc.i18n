Lokhu okukhethile kusikisela ukuthi ufuna ukunikezela umsebenzi wakho ekutheni usetshenziswe umphakathi noma ikanjani, idlelo lokwaziswa nenkulumo lapho kungekho okungokothile futhi yonke into ivumelekile.

Ukunikezela Imvume Emphakathini akuyona ilayisensi. Ngokusebenzisa lokhu, awusuke nje umane ushintsha okuthile elungelweni lakho lobunikazi; unikezela lonke ilungelo lakho lobunikazi emphakathini ngokungenamibandela. Lokhu kulinikezela kuhlala njalo futhi akuhlehliseki.
