Publisiering og deling af dit værk

Hvis du har markeret dit indhold korrekt, så vil søgemaskiner hjælpe andre med at finde det. Hertil kommer at en række store arkiver har udviklet teknikker, der identificerer Creative Commons indhols. Diss inkluderer:

Registries and Hosting

Common Content is a registry and directory of Creative Commons licensed works.
Archive.org's Open Source Audio and Open Source Movies offer a registry and free hosting for Creative Commons licensed audio and moving images.

Payment and Donation Systems

BitPass offers a micro-payment system that allows you to accept donations or payments for your work.
